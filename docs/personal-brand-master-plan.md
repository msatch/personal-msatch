# Personal Brand Master Plan (Technical/Business Consulting)

## 1. Objective
Build a high-conversion personal brand site in Spanish for LatAm prospects so one visitor can immediately understand your value, trust your expertise, and contact you for consulting engagements.

Primary business goal:
- Generate qualified consulting leads from founders, managers, and SMEs.

Primary product goal:
- Launch a modern, mobile-first landing page with an effective contact form and lead pipeline.

## 2. Target Market (Spanish / LatAm)

### Priority geographies
- Mexico
- Colombia
- Chile
- Peru
- Argentina

### ICP (Ideal Customer Profiles)
1. Founder / CEO (startup or growth-stage SMB)
- Needs strategic + technical execution guidance.
- Wants faster delivery with less risk.

2. Technical/Business Manager
- Needs prioritization, process, and architecture clarity.
- Wants measurable outcomes and predictable execution.

3. Traditional business owner in digital transition
- Needs practical modernization, not jargon-heavy consulting.
- Values trust, speed, and clear ROI.

### Core pains
- Slow product delivery and unclear priorities.
- Teams shipping without business alignment.
- Rework caused by weak planning and architecture.
- Lack of senior technical decision support.

### Buying triggers
- Team scaling pains.
- Failed/late delivery cycles.
- Need to launch/relaunch digital channels.
- Upcoming investor/client commitments.

## 3. Positioning and Brand Strategy

### Positioning statement
I help companies in LatAm convert business goals into technical execution with clarity, speed, and measurable outcomes.

### Differentiators
- Hybrid profile: technical + business management.
- Outcome-driven engagement model (not “hours for sale”).
- Strong execution discipline (prioritization, delivery cadence, quality gates).

### Brand voice
- Clear, direct, strategic, no unnecessary complexity.
- Senior but approachable.
- Practical and results-oriented.

## 4. Offer Architecture

### Core service lines
1. Strategic Technical Advisory
- Tech roadmap, architecture decisions, delivery strategy.

2. Delivery Acceleration
- Process optimization, backlog restructuring, execution rhythm.

3. Product & Business Alignment
- Align product decisions with revenue and operational KPIs.

4. Fractional Leadership Support
- Fractional CTO / Delivery Lead advisory layer.

### Entry offer (recommended)
- 45-minute diagnostic call + short action brief.

### Packaging model
- Starter: diagnostic + 2-week action plan.
- Growth: monthly advisory + execution oversight.
- Partner: embedded strategic support with team rituals and KPI tracking.

## 5. Landing Page Architecture

### Page structure
1. Hero
- Clear promise + who you help + primary CTA.

2. Problem/Solution
- Mirror common pain points and explain your method.

3. Services
- Outcome-based cards, each with scope and expected impact.

4. Work Method
- “How we work” in 3-5 steps.

5. Trust Section
- Bio, credentials, social proof, testimonials/case snippets.

6. FAQ
- Scope, timeline, pricing model, communication cadence.

7. Contact Section
- Fast form + alternate channels.

### CTA strategy
- Primary CTA repeated in hero, services, and footer.
- Secondary CTA for low-intent visitors (download brief / request intro call).

## 6. Copy and Messaging System

### Messaging pillars
- Clarity: simplify complexity.
- Execution: move from plans to outcomes.
- Business impact: focus on measurable results.

### Copy deliverables
- 3 headline variants.
- Full page copy in neutral Spanish.
- CTA microcopy variants.
- Objection handling snippets (budget, timeline, fit).

## 7. Design Direction (Modern, High-Credibility)

### UX principles
- Mobile-first.
- Fast scanning with strong hierarchy.
- Low friction to contact.

### Visual system
- Distinctive typography (not generic defaults).
- Controlled palette with clear contrast and professionalism.
- Intentional motion (section reveal, CTA emphasis) with restraint.

### Accessibility baseline
- AA contrast.
- Keyboard navigation.
- Semantic structure.
- Form labels/error states fully accessible.

## 8. Technical Implementation Plan

### Recommended stack
- Frontend: Next.js or Vite + React.
- Styling: Tailwind or scoped CSS system with design tokens.
- Forms: server action/API endpoint + validation.
- Hosting: Vercel/Netlify/Cloudflare Pages.

### Contact form requirements
- Required fields: name, email, company, objective, budget range (optional), message.
- Spam protection: honeypot + rate limiting + optional CAPTCHA.
- Delivery: email + CRM/Notion/Sheet webhook.
- UX: clear success/failure messages and response-time expectation.

### Data pipeline
- Form submit -> validation -> storage/logging -> notification -> follow-up workflow.

## 9. SEO and Content Strategy

### On-page SEO
- Keyword-aligned H1/H2 structure.
- Strong metadata + Open Graph.
- Schema markup (ProfessionalService / Person).

### Initial keyword clusters (Spanish)
- consultoria tecnologia
- consultor transformacion digital
- fractional cto latinoamerica
- estrategia tecnica para pymes
- optimizacion equipos desarrollo

### Supporting content roadmap
- Case-based articles.
- Practical guides for managers.
- Opinion pieces on delivery and digital strategy in LatAm.

## 10. Analytics and KPI Framework

### Tracking setup
- GA4 + GTM.
- Events: CTA click, form start, form submit, submit success.

### Core KPIs
- Visitor -> lead conversion rate.
- Qualified lead rate.
- Response time to new leads.
- Discovery-call booking rate.

### Cadence
- Weekly KPI review.
- Monthly optimization sprint.

## 11. Legal and Trust Layer

- Privacy notice and data consent in Spanish.
- Terms/conditions links in footer and form.
- Trust badges: credentials, logos, testimonials (when available).

## 12. QA, Performance, and Release Criteria

### Release checklist
- Responsive checks (mobile/tablet/desktop).
- Browser checks (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge).
- Lighthouse baseline (Performance/SEO/A11y).
- Form submission and notification tested end-to-end.

### Performance targets
- Fast LCP on mobile.
- Minimal JS payload.
- Optimized fonts and images.

## 13. 90-Day Go-to-Market Plan

### Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2): Foundation
- Finalize positioning, offers, and copy.
- Build MVP landing + form + analytics.

### Phase 2 (Weeks 3-6): Launch and signal generation
- Publish site.
- Start weekly LinkedIn authority posts.
- Run targeted outreach (warm network + referrals).

### Phase 3 (Weeks 7-12): Optimization and scale
- A/B test headline/CTA/form fields.
- Publish 2-4 high-value content pieces.
- Improve lead qualification and follow-up scripts.
